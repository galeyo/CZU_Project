AOC AGON AG272FCX

This is a curved gaming monitor with a diagonal of 27 inches and a resolution of Full HD, with a screen resolution of 16: 9, a radius of curvature of 1800 mm and the absence of "frames on the sides of the screen." The refresh rate and adaptive synchronization (Adaptive-Sync) ensure gamers a comfortable game, eliminating the so-called "gaps" of the image. Frame rate is twice as fast as other monitors. Thanks to frequent updates, you will have the opportunity to see all the shots, and you will be able to accurately strike at the enemy and appreciate the high-speed races in all their manifestations

Sony Alpha ILCE-A7R III

The advantages of the sony camera are really many, let's dwell only on some of them, since each user will be able to love this device for its own particularity. Superb picture and video quality. And this is not a trivial phrase, designed to attract buyers. The camera perfectly behaves as in shooting in normal conditions, and when working at night, with winds, minus temperatures and heat. High speed shooting, allowing you to do 10 frames per second. It turns out that sony a7r 3 is worth buying for fans of reporting or wedding photography, for which this mode is ideal. An advanced battery that holds a charge for much longer, which is important for on-site shooting. And thanks to the availability of appropriate connectors, the device can now be charged from the powerbank.
